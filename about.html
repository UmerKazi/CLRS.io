CLRS.IO

CLRS is a digital tool that generates a random colour palette. By utilizing the COLOURlovers API, a tool that gives you access to over 1.4 million named colors and more than 500,000 color palettes, clrs provides users with a virtually infinite amount of colour palettes and allows users to copy the HEX codes of the given colours. This tool is especially useful in a graphical design setting, allowing users to discover new shades of common colours to create beautiful designs.
